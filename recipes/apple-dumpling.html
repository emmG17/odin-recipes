Apple Dumpling

An apple a day keeps the doctor away, an apple dumpling a day keeps sadness at bay


Ingredients

  • 1 packet puff pastry sheets
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 6 large apples, peeled and diced
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 egg for brushing

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°/392°F
  2. Roll out pastry and cut it into 5" squares
  3. Grease a baking tray
  4. In a bowl mix the apples, spices, sugar, vanilla and half the butter
  5. Fill the pastries with the apple mix
  6. Fold the pastries into the center at all four corners and pinch in the centre
  7. Separate the dumplings on the baking tray
  8. Brush the dumplings with the remaining butter and egg
  9. Bake for 30-45 minutes, or until browned
