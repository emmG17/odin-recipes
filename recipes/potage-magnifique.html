Potage le Magnfique

This is an iconic dish to die for (at least in Skyrim), this french style soup will relieve your soul


Ingredients

  • 100 of butter
  • 1 large leek, chopped
  • 2 red onions, chopped
  • 5 cloves of garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup of white wine
  • 1 large potato, peeled and cubbed
  • 3 carrots, peeled and cubbed
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • ½ cup of plain flour
  • 1 tbsp mixed dried herbs
  • 1 bay leaf
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a pan and fry the leaks until softened
  2. Add the onions and garlic and fry until browned
  3. Add the wine and cook 2 minutes
  4. Add the potatoes, carrots, water, and broth
  5. Sift in the flour while stirring
  6. Add the herbs, salt and peper, and simmer for 30 mins
  7. Remove the bayleaf and blend the soup until smooth
