Sweetroll

Sometimes after slaying 15 dragons all you need is a sweet treat, this is the ultimate dessert Skyrim can offer


Ingredients

  • 1 large bundt tin
  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¾ cup of milk
  • ¾ cup of water
  • ½ cup of caster sugar
  • 1 cup of maple syrup
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 60g butter, melted
  • 1 vanilla pod, scraped
  • 3 tsp cinnamon powder
  • Glaze
    • 1 cup melted butter
    • 1 cup icing sugar
    • 1 vanilla pod, scraped

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/392°F
  2. Grease bundt tin
  3. Mix all the baking ingredients in a bowl
  4. Pour the batter into the bundt tin
  5. Bake for 45 minutes
  6. Flip the roll and cool it on a wire rack
  7. For the glaze, combine butter, sugar, and vanilla in a bowl and whip
  8. Drizzle the glaze over the top of the sweetroll
